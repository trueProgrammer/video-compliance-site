What the integration does

Compliance review where the review already happens.

Teams already use Frame.io for review and approval. VidComply surfaces compliance issues, territory flags, and QC findings inside that environment — with playback context and evidence — instead of redirecting operators to a separate tool.

All outputs are non-destructive. Issues are flagged with severity and category, human operators retain sign-off authority, and the outputs that leave the review step are usable directly in delivery, editorial, and downstream post handoff.

In-workflow compliance Issues surface inside Frame.io with playback context. No separate review tool, no context-switching.
Evidence-backed sign-off Every flagged issue includes the evidence trace operators need to make and document a compliance decision.
Non-destructive outputs All VidComply outputs are reviewable and reversible. Operators retain authority before anything commits to delivery.
Multi-territory profiles The same Frame.io project can be reviewed against different territory rulesets without duplicating the workflow.
Downstream handoff Compliance reports and QC sidecars leave Frame.io in formats usable by editorial, delivery, and legal teams immediately.
Minimal change management The integration requires no new review tool, no DAW replacement, and no change to the creative team's environment.
