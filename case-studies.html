Major Asian studio

Eliminating multi-track mic bleed in unscripted TV.

An anonymized audio-post deployment focused on reducing manual prep across dense dialogue sessions before final mix.

The problem

Senior audio engineers were spending large amounts of time manually routing dialogue, isolating tracks, and managing bleed before actual creative mixing could begin.

The solution

VidComply applied agentic automix and operator-facing audio prep to support multi-track routing, dialogue organization, and cleaner handoff into the mix stage.

The business impact

Lower prep overhead, faster session readiness, and less expensive senior-engineer time consumed by repetitive early-stage setup.

Global media provider

Dynamic compliance for regulated multi-market syndication.

An anonymized deployment focused on scaling highly regulated compliance review for geographically distributed audiences.

The problem

Manual QC and compliance review could not scale cleanly across regulated environments where rules change by territory and platform.

The solution

VidComply deployed its Dynamic Global Compliance Platform with operator review, profile-based workflows, and evidence-backed issue handling.

The business impact

Faster issue triage, improved operational consistency, and the ability to scale compliance checks across more markets without retraining the whole system for every rule change.

Catalog owner / distributor

Unlocking back-catalog remediation without reopening the entire post chain.

An anonymized operations story focused on making older libraries commercially usable again through simpler compliance and post workflows.

The problem

Legacy content libraries required too much manual review and coordination to be remediated for new territories, standards, or monetization channels.

The solution

VidComply used Compliance & QC, Post-Production Video, and reviewable outputs to identify where intervention was needed and route the right remediation path.

The business impact

More archive value surfaced, less manual investigative work, and a cleaner path to re-release or syndication readiness.
