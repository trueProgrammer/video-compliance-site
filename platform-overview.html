The four execution layers

These are the distinct parts of the platform that should be legible to enterprise buyers from the first conversation.

Compliance & QC

  • Scene-aware issue detection across picture, dialogue, and text
  • Evidence-backed issue review and QC sidecars
  • Human approval before downstream actions

Dynamic Global Compliance Platform

  • Profiles, issue queues, playback context, and operator actioning
  • Dynamic policy handling for different environments
  • One review surface across video, audio, and transcript signals

Post-Production Video

  • Cleanup and remediation planning
  • Timeline intelligence for editorial handoff
  • Outputs shaped for finishing workflows

Post-Production Audio

  • Agentic automix and bleed-aware routing
  • Dialogue prep for dense unscripted timelines
  • Faster prep before final mix and sign-off
