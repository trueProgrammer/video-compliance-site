What it is
VIDCOMPLY AI is a scene-understanding and compliance-filtering engine purpose-built for video training data pipelines. It scans raw video corpora at scale; detects and flags nudity, violence, branded materials, recognizable faces, copyrighted text, and culturally sensitive content; then enriches every clip with structured metadata—scene type, motion class, aesthetics score, and safety labels. The result is a clean, richly annotated dataset ready for model training, with full compliance provenance. Prevent liability at the data layer—not after your model generates it.

Training Data Solution Brief
Capabilities by job to be done
Content safety filtering
  • Detect and remove nudity, sexual content, graphic violence, self-harm, drugs, weapons, hate symbols, and gore across every frame
  • Configurable severity thresholds—keep borderline content or apply strict zero-tolerance policies per category
  • Child safety detection with highest-sensitivity defaults to prevent CSAM contamination
Brand, logo & IP detection
  • Identify logos, product placements, trademarked characters, and copyrighted text/signage in video and on-screen text
  • Optional redaction mode: blur or mask branded content to keep the clip while removing the IP risk
  • Custom brand lists for inclusion/exclusion; detect partial occlusions and rotated logos
Privacy & face filtering
  • Face detection and optional face-blurring for GDPR/CCPA compliance—individuals, crowds, minors
  • License plates, street signs, personal screens, and location identifiers flagged or redacted
  • Consent-list support: keep approved faces, filter everything else
Scene enrichment & metadata
  • 50+ attributes per clip: scene type, camera motion, shot scale, lighting, color palette, temporal coherence, and aesthetics score
  • Semantic captioning, action/object tags, and optical-flow-based motion classification for model conditioning
  • Near-duplicate detection and semantic clustering for deduplication and diversity balancing
Quality assessment & scale
  • Per-clip quality scoring: resolution, compression artifacts, framerate, blur, noise, and visual clarity
  • Watermark and overlay detection—strip synthetic text and platform HUDs before training
  • Horizontally scalable: process millions of clips per day via batch API or streaming pipeline connectors
📥 What Goes In
