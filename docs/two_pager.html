The Supply Chain Crisis

The Problem: The Vendor Management and Compliance Bottlenecks.
Video content is global by default, but the infrastructure is archaic. A single season launch requires thousands of unique assets: 16:9 trailers for TV, 9:16 shorts for TikTok, censored versions for the Middle East, and distinct metadata for Apple TV, Prime, and Roku.

The Execution Gap

Humans cannot edit fast enough to feed modern multi-platform demands. Studios pay agencies premium rates for mechanical tasks like resizing, re-encoding, and basic QC, draining budgets that should go to creativity.

The Compliance Trap

A single non-compliant frame (e.g., a vape pen in a kids' show in the UK) causes platform rejection, fines, and weeks of delays. Current solutions rely on manual spot-checks which are slow and error-prone.

The Product: An Autonomous Engine

VidComply is not a project management tool; it is a Generative Execution Layer. It connects to your existing storage (S3/Frame.io), analyzes the content, and autonomously creates the assets needed for delivery.

1. Compliance Core

  • Automated Edits: Detects and fixes compliance issues (blurring, bleeping, cutting) based on regional regulation databases.
  • Risk Scoring: Flags sensitive IP or legal concerns before files leave the studio.

2. Asset Factory

  • Generative Edit: Ingests raw footage to autonomously cut trailers and social shorts based on narrative pacing.
  • Visuals: Generates high-res posters, thumbnails, and localized metadata packages.

3. Distribution OS

  • Delivery: Provisions compliant packs for platforms (Apple, Amazon, FAST) automatically.
  • Lineage: Tracks every asset back to the source rights and legal clearance.

Competitive Landscape & Strategy

The "Netflix Gap"
Netflix spent billions building a proprietary "internal studio" to automate their global supply chain. They don't need VidComply because they built it themselves.

However, everyone else does. Traditional studios (Warner Bros, Paramount), Broadcasters (SJ ENM, BBC), and FAST operators do not have the engineering talent to build internal automation. They currently rely on a fragmented web of expensive vendors and agencies.

Our Strategy: We democratize Netflix-grade automation for the rest of the market.

  • vs. Agencies: We replace manual billing hours with instant, flat-rate software execution.
  • vs. Workflow Tools (Monday/Airtable): They track the status of the work; we do the work.
  • vs. GenAI Point Tools (Runway/Sora): They are creative brushes for artists; we are the industrial assembly line that creates the final deliverable package.

Why Now?

The cost of content *creation* is dropping toward zero thanks to GenAI, but the cost of *distribution* (compliance, packaging, legal) is rising due to platform complexity. VidComply captures the value in this gap. By building the proprietary dataset of global compliance rules and release specs, we are building the definitive operating system for the future of media.
