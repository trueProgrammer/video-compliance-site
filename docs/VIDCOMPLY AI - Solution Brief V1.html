What it is
VIDCOMPLY AI is a scene-understanding and policy engine with film-grade editorial automation. It detects risks across video, audio, and on-screen text; aligns them to platform and regional rules; then generates an edit plan with EDL/OTIO, masks, and audio cues or renders finished versions. Privacy blurs (faces, plates, signs), localization-safe edits, and ad-suitability adjustments are built in. Human review is supported at every step.

Solution Brief
Capabilities by job to be done
Compliance intelligence
  • Scene-level detection across violence, sex/nudity, minors, self-harm, drugs, weapons, hate, sensitive symbols, privacy and personal data, profanity, and on-screen text
  • Mapping to platform and regional frameworks with rule references and confidence
  • Rating and ad-suitability predictions with clear rationale
Editorial automation
  • One-click apply of blur, mute, and cut on exact timecodes
  • EDL/OTIO, masks, and audio cues for Resolve and Nuke; reversible, artist-editable results
  • Scene exclude or replace workflows for sensitive shots
Privacy and localization
  • Film-quality blurs for faces, number plates, signs, and screens; crowd de-identification
  • Region-specific variants that keep subtitles and M&E in sync
  • Timed-text conformance and checks for profanity, slurs, and cultural terms
Governance and integrations
  • Role-based approvals, change logs, and signed acceptance reports
  • REST API, webhooks, and storage connectors; handoff to IMF/Backlot-style workflows
  • Tunable policies, whitelists/blacklists, consent lists, and human-review queues
Accuracy and tuning
  • Thresholds and rules are tunable; confidence scores shown; human-in-the-loop for edge cases
